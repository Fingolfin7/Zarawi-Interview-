Welcome to Zarawi Trust (Al Zarawi)

Zarawi Trust, based in Zimbabwe is an emerging hybrid institution, setting itself up to operate as a hub for sustainable development in places and for women and young people identifying as rural. Through multidimensional approaches, Zarawi is mandated to embody in the form of different initiatives the key institutions crucial to sustainable development in any society identifying as rural or marginalised. Among them, a vibrant and independent think tank, a market-oriented enterprise, a humanitarian intervention, a capacity-building and skills development network and a voluntary workforce all of which whose work combined have the potential to eliminate socioeconomic inequality cycles affecting marginalised women and youth. Zarawi is a body recognised as a trust, based in Harare and operating with active projects in three provinces in the country.


To achieve our over-all mission, our entity is as a result active in, draws its strength from and into rural spaces and enterprises, academia, research and development, civic society, the public and the private sector to name a few. The Nexus Think Tank as the organisation's brain, with marginalised women and youth as its beating heart, we work through unconventional, intersectional approaches to tackle the grand challenges of the twenty-first century, in the hope that we can re-enfranchise the most disenfranchised populations in our generation. Our resolve guiding the way, Zarawi has no bounds to its ways of thinking and practicing towards sustainable development.
